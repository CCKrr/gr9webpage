Ravier's Journey

1st Quarter

During first quarter, I got close with people I weren't close with before. I surprised myself by making this small achievement in my social life.
Which is quite memorable since it's really heartwarming how I managed to easily connect with people as I continue being myself. I managed to make people around me smile every day even if sometimes the situation calls for a straight face, I'm just glad I managed to make new friends and be on good terms with people.
This quarter was the start of a wonderful journey of a year long bonding with my classmates.

2nd Quarter

This quarter made me smile, genuinely. I managed to get another line of 9 in my report card which may seem small but feels big for me, like it's a big achievement for me to achieve a new milestone on my report card.
In this quarter, I was more engaged with trying in my academic life, like actually participating properly in group projects and constantly asking for guidance in solo projects to get something done. Getting another line of 9 in my report card was a memorable achievement that I will forever treasure, using it to achieve another one.
One small step at a time to the top.

3rd Quarter

In this quarter or rather during intramurals in this quarter, me and my classmates managed to get in champions in basketball. Brings back memories when me and my previous classmates brought the champion trophy home to our section and our advisor.
This is another achievement that allowed us to shine more during intramurals, as this victory crowned us as intramurals champion. I thank all of my classmates efforts into bringing their own victory and our victory.
Whether their sport may be physical, board-games, or mind-games, they deserve to be applauded.

4th Quarter

During this quarter, I made another academic achievement. But, this time I'm not alone in my circle that made an academic achievement.
Me and my friends got 40+ scores in our exams, once again. This made me happy since me and my friends are improving and thriving even in small ways and steps because it means I'm not alone in my journey on improving.
I hope this becomes a consistent thing as we work and try even if the impact or effect isn't that noticeable and big.

As the year ends...

As the school year comes to an end, I experienced and went through my own challenges, highs, lows, and times where it's deeper than low. Though, despite it, I will always bring positivity to me and everyone around me, I know that even my simple laughs and theatrics can have a big impact on their day and mood.
I'm glad that I am with people who do not judge or do not ruin the fun over their desire for negativity, and I'm glad that throughout this year, I have my friends beside me. I wish that in the next school year, it's also like this but more livelier and engaging for each and everyone of us.
I hope for the best for everyone that I have met and haven't met.

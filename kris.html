Kristine's Journey

1st Quarter

In first quarter, I was still nervous and shy around my new classmates yet we got along just fine. During first quarter, we learned about our section's saint which is the story of Saint Maria Goretti, I couldn't really forget about her story and how she made me think quite differently about people.
Like how I should be more forgiving with people despite their shortcomings or mistakes against me, this first quarter already had a big impact on me because it already challenged me in brainstorming for answers. I also made new friends in this quarter real fast which helped me improve in my communication skills and critical thinking in different situations with them.
Yet, this is only first quarter and it had already improved some of my skills and strengths, as well as improving my weakness which is being unable to talk in front of people without stuttering or shying away.

2nd Quarter

In second quarter, I've been exposed to even more drawing which I was nervous about messing up during the process but managed to finish it well. During this quarter, I experienced a lot of new challenges like to be consistently drawing because of art scaffolds which I love and hate due to how time consuming some of the references are.
Although, this quarter improved my artistic skills even more by requiring me to actually learn how to align elements within my drawing. Felt a bit proud since in this quarter, my grades kept going up in my subjects even if my average isn't exactly moving but it is, in decimals though.
This quarter tested my patience and discipline the most which I'm happy about since it worked.

3rd Quarter

In third quarter, I made a few achievements which I'm proud of. One of the few achievements I achieved is that, I managed to win for grade 9 Word Factory after 2 years of joining it and beating one of the people that I thought would crush me mercilessly in Scrabble.
I also joined a Bible Quiz Bee and me and my group got into the 4th place, I do wish I could've done better but it is what it is and I commend myself for actually reviewing and trying rather just winging it like usual. Though, this quarter tested my patience more than the previous quarter since deadlines on top of deadlines was frying my brain (mentally and metaphorically).
I'm happy in this quarter too, since I'm improving in Math like, I'm no longer getting under 34 scores but almost to 40+ scores.

4th Quarter

This 4th quarter, everything felt fast and rushed to be honest. Though, I did enjoy foundation as I witnessed one of my friends perform Sailor Song by Gigi Perez, as emotional as it sounds, I shed a tear when I heard her sing it.
I also experienced the pressure of short deadlines of absolutely time consuming projects. But I realized.. it can also be a lesson of having to be efficient and swift while being consistent with performing the best of myself.
I had my own struggles, ups, and downs during this quarter since failed 2-4 times in English but I manage to catch up in the activity for second half.

As the year ends...

As the school year ends, I remember that this year wasn't so bad despite the setbacks that I experienced, may it be academical or personal. I learned a lot from people; friends, classmates, teachers, and strangers, they taught and showed me me different things, like their own opinions, perspectives, morals, thoughts on things, and most especially who they are.
I thank the people who have trusted me with their feelings and allow me to help them in times of need. I'm glad I'm surrounded by caring, thoughtful, helpful, and lively people who don't let the dark in their life consume them.
As this wonderful and definitely memorable year, I wish that every will be blessed by God and I hope that they reach the dreams that they long for.

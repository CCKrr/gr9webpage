Kristine's Journey

1st Quarter

In first quarter, I was still nervous and shy around my new classmates yet we got along just fine. During first quarter, we learned about our section's saint which is the story of Saint Maria Goretti, I couldn't really forget about her story and how she made me think quite differently about people.
Like how I should be more forgiving with people despite their shortcomings or mistakes against me, this first quarter already had a big impact on me because it already challenged me in brainstorming for answers. I also made new friends in this quarter real fast which helped me improve in my communication skills and critical thinking in different situations with them.
Yet, this is only first quarter and it had already improved some of my skills and strengths, as well as improving my weakness which is being unable to talk in front of people without stuttering or shying away.

2nd Quarter

In second quarter, I've been exposed to even more drawing which I was nervous about messing up during the process but managed to finish it well. During this quarter, I experienced a lot of new challenges like to be consistently drawing because of art scaffolds which I love and hate due to how time consuming some of the references are.
Although, this quarter improved my artistic skills even more by requiring me to actually learn how to align elements within my drawing. Felt a bit proud since in this quarter, my grades kept going up in my subjects even if my average isn't exactly moving but it is, in decimals though.
This quarter tested my patience and discipline the most which I'm happy about since it worked.
